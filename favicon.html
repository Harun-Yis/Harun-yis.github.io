Favicon with "H" for Harun

This page shows how your new favicon will look. The "H" represents "Harun" and uses your website's accent color (#0563bb).

To replace your current favicon:

  1. Right-click on the blue "H" icon above
  2. Select "Save image as..."
  3. Save it as "favicon.png" in your "assets/img/" folder
  4. Replace the existing favicon.png file

Alternative method:

You can also use online favicon generators like favicon.io or realfavicongenerator.net to create a proper favicon from the "H" design.
